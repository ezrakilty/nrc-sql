Weaken a typing derivation by extending its environment and it still holds.
Lemma Weakening :
  ∀ env' tm ty env, Typing env tm ty →
    Typing (env++env') tm ty.

Special case of weakening for closed terms.
Lemma Weakening_closed :
  ∀ tm ty env, Typing nil tm ty → Typing env tm ty.

#[export]
Hint Resolve Weakening_closed.
